About Me

Hello, my name is Hadia and I am a current Graphic design student pursuing a Bachelors degree at Virginia Commonwealth University with an Associates degree in Visual Arts. I have five years of experience in design and about a year and a half of experience in social media managing. I am proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Indesign, and Canva. I also take my own photography with my Canon t6i for in person work. For fun, I love to spend time in nature, listen to music, and of course create arts and crafts.
